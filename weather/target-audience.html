The target audience are male and females ages 30 to 65 years old.

Scenarios

Scenario #1

Gilgore is taking his class on a field trip to a local pond. He needs to know if what the weather would be like in the coming week to plan for which day to have the field trip.

Scenario #2

Linda's child has a soccer game today after work. She needs to check the weather to see if she needs to bring an umbrella or not.

Scenario #3

Gilgore is going camping with his family on labor day weekend. He needs to see what the weather will be like to see if he needs to bring extra coats or not.

Scenario #4

Linda is having a open house on a property where the highlight is the backyard and needs to make sure the weather will be beautiful to help present the house better.

Scenario #5

Gilgore walks his dog everyday after work and checks the weather every morning to see what his walk will be like and if he needs to expect rain or not.byui
